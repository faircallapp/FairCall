The classroom participation problem

Teachers want fairness. But in the flow of a lesson, bias creeps in.

Same hands, every time

A few confident voices dominate. The rest stop trying.

Quiet students disappear

Not intentionally overlooked — just hard to notice in a busy class.

No reliable record

Without data, you can't see the pattern — or fix it.

How FairCall solves it

Random fair picks

One tap to select a student. Cooldown prevents back-to-back repeats. Rounds ensure everyone gets a turn before anyone goes twice.

One-tap participation logging

Engaged, Tried, No response, Skip. Tag each interaction instantly — no forms, no paperwork.

Session insights + history

See participation spread at a glance. Track trends over days or weeks. Export data when you need it.
