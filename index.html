Why it matters

In most classes, a few voices dominate. Quiet students disappear — not intentionally, just by habit. FairCall makes participation visible, so you can balance it.

Sound familiar?

  • Dominant volunteers. Same hands every time.
  • Quiet students overlooked. Hard to notice who hasn’t spoken.
  • Hard to track fairly. No reliable record of who got called.

FairCall helps.

  • Equity mode. Weighted toward students called less.
  • Rounds + cooldown. Everyone gets a turn before repeats.
  • Fast logging + analytics. See participation spread at a glance.
